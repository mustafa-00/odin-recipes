Bolani

Back to Main page

Bolani is an afghani traditional food

ingredients

  • potato
  • leek
  • flour
  • vegetables

Steps

  1. you need to prepare the flour
  2. you need to prepare the vegetables
  3. then you need to prepare the potato
  4. you need to mix the vegetables with potato
  5. make the flour in circle shapes
  6. fill in the circles with the potatoes
  7. put them in to the hot oil to be cooked
