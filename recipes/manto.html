manto

Manto is an afghani traditional food mostly filled in with meatballs.

ingredients

  • meatballs
  • flour
  • vegetables

Steps

  1. you need to prepare the flour
  2. you need to prepare the vegetables
  3. then you need to prepare the meatballs
  4. you need to mix the vegetables with meatballs
  5. make the flour in square shapes
  6. fill in the squares with the meatballs
  7. put them in their special dishes on the fire
